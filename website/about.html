About

Background on Vischeck, and this repo’s browser-based rebuild.

Vischeck is a webpage for simulating and correcting color vision deficiencies. It was originally launched in 2000 by Robert Dougherty and Alex Wade. In those days we were both post-docs in a neuroscience lab at Stanford. Bob now works for Mindstrong and Alex is at the University of York.

This is a rebuild of the original site. We’ve switched to a client-side JavaScript implementation.

In the meantime, code from the original Vischeck simulation is now in GIMP. There are also other great simulators like Coblis and Toptal’s Colorfilter.
